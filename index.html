Dr. Girish Vadgaonkar

About the Doctor

Dr. Vadgaonkar Girish Vitthalrao is specialized in Internal Medicine. He completed his MBBS from King Edward Memorial Hospital and Seth Gordhandas Sunderdas Medical College in 2008. Then he did his MD in General Medicine from the Maharashtra University of Health Sciences in 2011. Dr. Girish currently practices at Shriniwas Hospital, Maharashtra, with 14 years of clinical experience. But his latest endeavour has been owning and operating Deopur Hospital as CEO.

Experience:
- Assistant professor in BJ Medical college
- Intensivist in Sahyadri Super Speciality Hospital Pune
- Medical director of Shriniwas Hospital for 4 years in Nashik
- Visiting consultant and intensivist in Magnum Cardiac Institute
- Full time consultant in Global Hospital Mumbai
- During the COVID wave Dr. Girish served humanity by being the first responder in the crisis
- Established fever OPD in BSES Global Hospital
- One of the first physicians to work for COVID patients in a global hospital when other hospitals were shut down
- Served as a Covid Consultant to government institutes; Bharat Ratna Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Municipal Corporation Hospital Kandivali
- Worked as a Covid Consultant in NESCO COVID Center to serve patients
- Learned 2D Echo from various stalwarts in the field such as Dr. Lotlikar
